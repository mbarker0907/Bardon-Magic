Classical Elements Visualization

--- Akasha (Ether) ---
The quintessence or spiritual force behind the physical world.
Governs space and the processes of transformation.
Represents the principle of consciousness.

--- Fire ---
Element of willpower and transformation.
Represents warmth and expansion.
Governs passion, courage, and enthusiasm.

--- Water ---
Element of emotion, intuition, and fluidity.
Represents coldness and contraction.
Governs feelings, love, and devotion.

--- Air ---
Element of intellect, wisdom, and clarity.
Represents hotness and moisture.
Governs communication, thought, and logic.

--- Earth ---
Element of solidity, stability, and endurance.
Represents coldness and dryness.
Governs material matters, practicality, and reliability.
